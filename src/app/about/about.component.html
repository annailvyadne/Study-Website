About StudySurface

StudySurface is designed to help students manage their study time more effectively. Our goal is to provide tools and resources to enhance productivity and make studying a more organized and efficient process. With features like task management, Pomodoro timers, and study resources, StudySurface is your companion in achieving academic success.

About the Developer

Hello! I'm Lianne, the web developer behind StudySurface. With a passion for creating tools that aid in productivity and learning, I designed StudySurface to be a practical solution for students like me looking to optimize their study habits. I hope you find it helpful in your academic journey.
